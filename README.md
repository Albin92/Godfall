Problem Statement:
Elderly individuals often struggle with managing their medications on time, monitoring their health vitals, and maintaining independent living — especially when living alone or away from family. Missed medications, undetected health changes, and lack of accessible digital tools contribute to medical emergencies and reduced quality of life for senior citizens.

Proposed Solution:
MediCare+ is an accessible and proactive digital health companion designed specifically for elderly users. The solution provides:
Medication Reminders – Smart, medication schedules with dosage tracking.
Health Monitoring Dashboard- Track vitals like heart rate, blood pressure, and SpO2 in real-time.
Emergency SOS – One-tap emergency alert system that notifies caregivers and family members instantly.
Caregiver Portal – A dedicated interface for family members and doctors to remotely monitor health status.

Innovation & Creativity:
What makes MediCare+ unique:
Non-intrusive design – Minimal data entry required from the elderly user; most data is pre-configured by caregivers.
Senior-friendly: UI with large buttons and easy navigation
One stop preview:One stop health vitals preview in homepage
